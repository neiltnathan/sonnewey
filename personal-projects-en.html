Personal Work - A painting or photo on a wall can provide quite refuge in a house, they can bring a pleasing ambience. And the nice part is that you have a unique exemplar hanging on your wall. Not something that anyone could have purchased from the shopping centre.

“I try to photograph everyday objects in a fascinating or interesting way. I have learned an enormous amount about nature, ever since I started taking photos (of nature).” And this you can see in my photos. Have you ever looked intensively at a leaf or a tree? Or at the mist? Or at an egg?

A well taken photo helps you observe nature or contemporary objects that at first you just look at and now unexpectedly get your attention.

There are many ways to display your photo on the wall. A beautiful frame, with or without passe-partout, on canvas, plexiglass, big or small, maybe you have a display idea?

On this site you will see examples of the photographers’ personal work. Photos of subjects, things, objects that are taken in a way that is unique to the photographer. If you look through his eyes at the pictures he sees, everyday things often look better.
