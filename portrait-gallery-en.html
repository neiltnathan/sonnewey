Portrait - How do you want to look on a photo? Why do you want a photo of yourself? Or of your husband, your wife, your son or daughter. A portrait photo is a very personal and intimate capture of the person, of the character and feeling. A good portrait photo can provide energy, to yourself or to others that receive a photo. You are not overly vain if you want a portrait photo of yourself. You are in a good moment in your life that you want to hold onto. For now. For later. Also, in business it is important that you have a good profile photo. Think to your Linked In profile. Or a good photo of yourself in your email signature closing info.

By listening to your wishes and translating those to the correct technique and materials can a good photographer capture the desired feel and correct character in a photo. And sometimes, maybe give just that little bit extra so that you can look back with pride at the photo.

It is fantastic to give a photo of yourself to a loved one or to capture your loved one on a photo that you both can look at for a long time.
